IoT Clouds



IoT Cloud is a platform, that is designed to store and process Internet of Things (IoT) data. IoT platforms helps in connecting and extracting data from a potentially vast number and variety of endpoints, which are sometimes in inconvenient locations with spotty connectivity. Its critical for connected business, connecting millions of devices, managing the real-time data they produce.
